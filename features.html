Features

 Home / Features
Some of the most important technical and functional features of Tryton includes

100% API

Already have a software component running on your system and want to integrate the same with Tryton?

Don’t worry! Tryton provides 100% API Integration, which helps software developers and vendors to bundle their own software with one or more Tryton modules.

With just a matter of clicks, you can have all your data and functionality already saved in your system, ready to be migrated to Tryton Solution! Integrating new applications and softwares with the existing is a piece of cake with the help of API feature in Tryton.


Highly Customizable

Every business is different and so should be the solution to its problems.

Tryton is like water. You can literally do anything with the system to redesign and mold it as per your needs. Tryton is not a system that can be extended, it can be designed. Due to its high flexibility you can keep your system minimalistic by keeping only those features that you need at the moment. This implies that you spend your budget on customization rather than buying licences, thus providing full value for your money.

If your business model is likely to change as a result of market adaptations or strategic decisions, investing in Tryton will prove to be an intelligent decision in a long run.

Tryton grows as your business grows. What you want your system to do, it’ll learn and do it for you.

Fast

With Tryton acting as the core of the system, all your tasks can be managed with simple clicks, getting results in the blink of an eye!

The Tryton kernel is ELEVEN TIMES FASTER, which means it can handle huge applications featuring colossal data sets at lightning fast speed. You spend more time in getting actual work done, rather than wasting your time on slow systems.

Its speed is not only limited to users, but it is also a boon for developers. Clean codes written in the tryton core makes development faster, implying that your tailored project will be ready in much less time than you’d expect it to.


Modular

Your business doesn't need the complete range of functionality available in a typical ERP suite.

Offered in a modular format, Tryton gives you the freedom to choose the modules you need today, rather than overfilling your plate with useless modules. You get to choose from a large number of vigorously tested and stable modules that can be implemented in various combinations.

Each module is focused on one area of business processes, such as product development, marketing, accounting, etc. which can be linked to the common database, so that all of the information between the departments is accessible in real time.

You can consider Tryton as LEGO pieces. You can make a ship, a car, a gun, a home or whatever you want to make by connecting different pieces.


Open Source

If you are searching for an Open source ERP system, it means that you are already aware of the advantages on an Open source system.

As Tryton is an open source ERP system, you can easily play around with the code and tweak it to suit your needs. With the help of people with right skillset, you can do wonders with your ERP solution.

The lack of licensing fees lowers your cost of investment. Whenever a new version is available, if you do decide to upgrade, there is no new licence fee to be paid. This helps in saving you money by avoiding upgrades that you don’t need.

Scalable

Tryton is a scalable ERP system which means that it does not lose its efficiency when the system’s complexity increases.

As your business grows you save a lot of money by saving yourself from unnecessary reinvestments in the ERP system. Even when the amount of data and users increases vastly in your system, Tryton handles it with ease.

Being platform independent, Tryton can adapt to any platform that you use for your business. Also, if you wish to change the platform in future, you do not have to worry about its compatibility with Tryton.

In short, investing your money in Tryton is a smart move as it makes your ERP investment future proof.

Secure

Tryton is one of the most secure ERP systems, that even commercial ERP systems fall short in front of Tryton’s security.

At first, it may seem that an open source system might be an unsecure system as it’s code is open to all. But it is a big common misconception. Open source softwares are heavily tested as compared to their commercial counterparts. Being an uncountable number of downloaders of the Open Source ERP system, the feedbacks received by the community is huge. Once a flaw is reported, a patch is developed and suggested by the community to fix the flaw as soon as possible.

Tryton was primarily designed to avoid every possible security threat and the Tryton community treats security of user information with utmost importance. Tryton uses salted hash passwords which ensures that your passwords are encrypted with the best industry practice.

Stable

Stability is not a feature, rather a necessity for an ERP system.

You really don’t want to lose your business or potential clients, just because your system gets down every other time because of stability issues.

And if your business has a need to be up and live 24*7, then rightly investing in a stable ERP system is the first priority.

Tryton provides an extremely stable ERP system for small and midsized businesses without the hassle of maintenance.

The release cycle for Tryton have always been consistent which fortifies system’s stability with every update. The Database Management System used in Tryton is PostGreSQL, which is considered as one of the most stable Management system.

Moreover, Tryton can be expanded horizontally if you wish to increase the server space.

This leads to server redundancy which means that your system will remain up and running, even if one of your servers is down.
