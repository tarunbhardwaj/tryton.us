  • What is Tryton?

    Tryton is a suite of business applications for small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs) and organizations to manage and optimize business processes.

    OR Technically,

    Tryton Is a three-tiers high-level general purpose application platform under the license GPL-3 written in Python and using PostgreSQL as database engine.

    Sounds like some alien language?

    See this video and you will know what we are talking about.

Open Source

As Tryton is an open source ERP system, you can easily play around with the code and tweak it to suit your needs. With the help of people with right skillset, you can do wonders with your ERP solution.

Modular

Offered in a modular format, Tryton gives you the freedom to choose the modules you need today, rather than overfilling your plate with useless modules.

100% API

Tryton provides 100% API Integration, Integrating new applications and softwares with the existing is a piece of cake with the help of API feature in Tryton.
