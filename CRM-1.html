Customer Relationship Management

Attract, Retain and Delight More Customers with Tryton’s CRM. It entails all aspects of interaction that you have with a customer, whether it is sales or service-related.

Know your customers

How can you serve your customer well when you don’t know about the customer?

CRM provides you with the customer business data that helps you to understand your customer much better, know what the customer products are and what the customer needs.


Convert leads into Opportunities

CRM helps you to track the status of every lead so that you don’t have to remember who’s who of every lead. By being active and pro-active with the help of CRM, it’s extremely easy to concentrate more on business strategies and successfully convert lead into opportunities and expand your business .


Retain Current Customers

It’s important to expand your business and get more customers working but with you, but it is equally important to retain the existing ones. Providing the best service to the customer helps your company make a goodwill in the market and well who’s not happy to see his customers happy!


Up-selling was never so easy!

Business is not only about roping in more customers but also analysing what more can be sold to the existing customer. CRM assists you in knowing in and outs of your customer, helps you analyse what more can be offered to the existing customers and thus create more Up-sell opportunities.


Decrease Customer Management costs

You don’t need to assign an individual Account Manager to the every customer when you’ve got CRM. Tryton’s CRM has excellent features which provide quick and easy options to manage multiple customers by a single person.
