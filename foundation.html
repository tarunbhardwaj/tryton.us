TRYTON SOFTWARE FOUNDATION

The Tryton Foundation is a private foundation (Fondation Privée) under the belgian law.
It aims to protect, promote and develop the free software Tryton.

aims to develop and support conferences, meetings and community activities

holds and administers the tryton.org infrastructure

organizes the community of supporters

manages and promotes the Tryton trademark

Aside from the community support, Tryton project has several companies or partners which provide professional services which include its implementation, customizations, integrations with other software and support, maintenance services.
